HOW TO PLAY

Pæir a Pæra · Treasure Vault

ENTRY COST

Each run costs 5 coins. You start with a fresh 5×5 vault every time.

MEMORISE PHASE

All 25 tiles reveal for 200 ms — memorise the positions. Then they flip back and shuffle.

2-MINUTE CLOCK

Flip tiles before the vault seals. When time runs out all remaining tiles auto-reveal and your haul is totalled.

PRIZES

💎 Diamond ₱1,000 · 💰 Gold Bag ₱500 · 💵 Cash ₱100 · 💣 Bomb nothing · 🔥 Fire −₱500. Earnings go to your Bank.

PRIZES & HAZARDS

💎 ₱1,000 · 💰 ₱500 · 💵 ₱100 · 🧨 −₱10 · 💣 −₱50 · 💸 −₱100 + cancels ❄️. Wave 10+: 🪙 ₱300 replaces 💎.

❄️
FREEZE ❄️

Pauses countdown 15s and grants immunity to 🧨 and 💣. Warning: 💸 ignores immunity and cancels your freeze entirely.

⭐
STAR ⭐

Doubles all money earned for 25 seconds. Getting another ⭐ while active adds 25 more seconds — duration stacks, multiplier never drops.

🌟
SUPERSTAR 🌟

Rare upgrade — 4× money for 25 seconds. If ⭐ is already active, the multiplier upgrades to ×4 and duration stacks.

🧿
ORB 🧿

Briefly reveals all hidden tiles for 2 seconds so you can memorise the board before they flip back.

MYSTERY GIFT 🎁

Extremely rare (1% per wave). Floats to your Gift Tray — tap it after the vault seals for 100–500 coins.

BANK → COINS

Every ₱5,000 in your bank converts to 50 coins. Cash out any time from the accordion below the board.
