Fruit Game

Free to Play · Luck-Based · Arcade
LIVE

A retro arcade slot game where luck is everything. Sign in with Google, grab your 100 free starting credits, and start spinning. Each spin costs 5 credits — land on a fruit to collect its value, or hit a 💣 bomb and walk away with nothing. Simple. Addictive. Free.

What keeps it fun The Tension Loop — every spin costs 5 but most wins return less than 5, so your credits slowly drain unless you hit a pineapple or better. That pressure is what makes each spin feel meaningful. You're always one good spin away from recovering.
The Jackpot Moment — landing 🍫 at ₱100 for a 5-credit bet is a 20x return. It's rare enough to feel genuinely exciting when it happens.
The Bomb — losing everything on a spin feels worse than gaining the equivalent feels good. That asymmetry is what makes the bomb psychologically effective without being unfair.
Daily Rewards — every 24 hours players get +100 free credits, meaning even if they go broke they can come back tomorrow. This removes frustration and keeps people returning.
The Leaderboard — credits accumulate globally, so there's a social layer. Players can see where they rank against everyone else, giving long-term motivation beyond individual spins.
The core design philosophy 🧿 The game is built around low barrier, high replayability. Sign in once with Google, get credits instantly, start spinning. No tutorials, no menus, no friction. The spin animation (~4 seconds of the highlight traveling around the grid) builds anticipation without feeling slow. The sound design — ticks during the spin, a win tone, a low bomb sound — adds sensory feedback that makes outcomes feel satisfying or dramatic.
It's essentially a modern retro slot machine — the aesthetic (Bungee font, dark arcade palette, glowing cells) evokes classic arcade machines, but it runs entirely in the browser with real-time data synced to Firestore, so your credits, history, and leaderboard rank are persistent across devices and sessions.
Who it's for Anyone who enjoys casual games with a luck element — the kind you play for 5 minutes during a break. It's not skill-based, which lowers the entry barrier completely. The ₱ currency framing gives it a sense of real stakes without actual money involved.
🎯
How to Win The highlight travels the border grid and stops on a random fruit. Its value is added to your credits.
⚖️
Weighted Odds Not all fruits are equal. Cherries are common, the jackpot bar is rare. Every spin is a calculated gamble.
🎁
Daily Rewards Claim +100 free credits every 24 hours. Go broke? Come back tomorrow — the game always gives you another shot.
🏆
Leaderboard Your credits persist globally. Climb the ranks, hold the top spot, and let everyone know who spins best.

Pop Match

Free to Play · Skill-Based · Memory Arcade
LIVE

A fast-paced memory arcade game where skill pays — literally. Sign in with Google, flip face-down cards to reveal hidden symbols, and match pairs before the clock runs out. No credits required to play. Instead, clearing stages earns you credits you can spend in other games. Five escalating stages. One combo chain. How far can you go?

What keeps it fun The Combo Chain — every consecutive match without a miss multiplies your score. Your first match scores 12 pts, but by your fifth consecutive match you're scoring 60 pts for the same action. One miss resets the chain to zero, which makes each flip feel genuinely tense.
The Memory Window — when you flip a card that doesn't match, both cards stay visible for 680ms before flipping back. That brief window is where the game is actually won or lost. Players who use it to memorize positions will dramatically outperform players who don't.
The Stage Ladder — each stage adds more pairs and cuts the timer, so the pressure compounds. Clearing Stage 1 feels easy. Clearing Stage 5 under a 43-second clock with 12 pairs on a 5×5 grid feels like a genuine achievement.
Credit Rewards — unlike Fruit Game, Pop Match costs nothing to play. It's the grind-and-earn counterpart — skilled players can earn up to 45 credits across a full 5-stage run, directly fueling their Fruit Game sessions.
The core design philosophy 🧿 Pop Match is built around the tension between speed and accuracy. The timer pushes you to move fast. The combo system punishes recklessness. These two forces are always pulling against each other, and finding your personal rhythm between them is where the skill ceiling lives.
It's designed as the deliberate counterweight to Fruit Game — where Fruit Game is pure luck and passive tension, Pop Match is pure skill and active pressure. The two games share a credit economy but serve completely different player moods. On a lucky day you spin. On a focused day you match.
The arcade aesthetic — dark palette, glowing cards, retro sound design — is shared with the rest of the platform intentionally. Everything feels like it belongs in the same cabinet.
Who it's for Players who want to feel like their result was earned, not rolled. If Fruit Game frustrates you because luck is out of your hands, Pop Match is the antidote — every outcome here traces directly back to a decision you made. It also rewards returning players more than new ones, since spatial memory of symbol positions improves with familiarity.
🧠
How to Win Flip two cards per turn. Match the symbols to keep them face-up. Clear all pairs before the timer hits zero to advance to the next stage. Miss a pair and both cards flip back face-down.
⚡
Combo Multiplier Consecutive matches multiply your score. Chain them together for massive points. One miss resets the chain — so every flip carries real stakes.
💰
Earn Credits Pop Match is free to play and pays out credits on every stage cleared. Max 50 credits earnable per day — spend them on Fruit Game spins or save them up.
🏆
High Score Board Your personal best is saved to your account and appears on the global Pop Match leaderboard on the dashboard. Score is determined by combo chains and stage completion bonuses.
